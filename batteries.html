BATTERIES

Univercell Valve Regulated Lead Acid (VRLA) & AGM Batteries

We Supply VRLA and AGM types as our industry standard battery for UPS and Inverter solutions. The reason is simple, premium quality, unmatched reliability, long life and advanced engineering with years of application expertise. This is at the heart of what we are doing here in Ghana since 2014. Our UNIVERCELL brand battery is well-known for its stable and reliable performance. The Battery can withstand overcharge, longer discharge, vibration and shock conditions. It is also capable of extended storage time compare to the traditional batteries. UNIVERCELL's unique construction and sealing technique guarantees that no electrolyte leakage can occur from the terminals of case. This feature ensures safe and efficient operation of UNIVERCELL batteries in any position.


Univercell batteries are equipped with a safe low-pressure venting system, which operates from 1 psi to 6 psi. The venting system is designed to release excess gas in the event that the gas pressure rises to level above the normal rate. Afterwards, the venting system automatically re-seals itself when the gas pressure level returns to it is normal rate. This feature prevents excessive build-up of gas in the batteries. This low-pressure venting system coupled with the extraordinary high recombination make our batteries the safest sealed lead-acid batteries available.
